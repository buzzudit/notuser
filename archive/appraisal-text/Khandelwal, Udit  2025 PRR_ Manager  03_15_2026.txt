Areas for Development
As Udit continues to pioneer AI transformation and explore a hybrid IC/Director path, the following
development areas will be critical for expanding his impact and creating future career opportunities.
Building these capabilities will position him well for whatever opportunities emerge as the organization
evolves.
Develop Product and Business Acumen
To expand his influence and impact, Udit should invest in understanding athena's products, their strategy,
how the business makes decisions, and our customers and their needs. This foundation is essential for
contributing meaningfully to strategic discussions and making informed decisions about where to focus
innovation efforts. He should spend time learning product strategy deeply, understanding customer
workflows and pain points through direct engagement, studying how the business evaluates opportunities
and makes tradeoffs, and developing fluency in the language of business outcomes.
This could look like: Shadowing product leaders to understand strategy development, conducting customer
visits or interviews to understand motivations and challenges and advocating for unmet client needs, and
participating in business planning discussions to understand how decisions are made.
